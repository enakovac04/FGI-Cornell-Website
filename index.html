About Us

The Cornell Chapter of First Generation Investors is dedicated to providing first-generation students with the opportunity to learn investing fundamentals in an accessible and supportive environment.

Our Mission

Empowering the next generation of investors through financial education, practical experience, and community support at Cornell University. We equip students with the skills and knowledge to create lasting financial impact and navigate the world of investing.
