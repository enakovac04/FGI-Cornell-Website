OUR MISSION

First Generation Investors (FGI) empowers first-generation college students through financial literacy. We aim to provide practical financial education and investment knowledge, ensuring that students are equipped with skills to navigate and succeed in their financial futures.

At the Cornell Chapter, we foster a supportive community, bridging financial education with hands-on experiences through mentorship and real-world applications. We aim to create pathways for financial independence and success, inspiring the next generation of informed and empowered leaders.
